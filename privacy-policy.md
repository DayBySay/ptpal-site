# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 30, 2024**

## Overview

PTPal ("the App") is a macOS application for importing photos from PTP-compatible cameras. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

The App only accesses:
- PTP camera data (photos and metadata) connected via USB
- Local file system for saving imported photos

## How We Use Your Information

Your photos and metadata are used **exclusively** for:
- Displaying thumbnails and EXIF information
- Copying files to your selected destination folder

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only
- **All data remains on your Mac**
- No data is transmitted to external servers
- No analytics or tracking is implemented

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

- No third-party analytics services
- No advertising networks
- No external servers or databases

## Your Rights

You can:
- Choose which photos to import
- Select your preferred destination folder
- Delete imported photos at any time

## Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/DayBySay/PTPal/issues).
